Detailed Production Manipulations and Technical Requirements

The video was produced using Microsoft Clipchamp and specifically utilized the following techniques to meet all project technical specifications:

  • Core Media & Structure

    Utilized 14 high-resolution still images (sourced from Pexels/Unsplash) and two audio tracks: a self-recorded voiceover (narration) and a licensed, lower-volume background music track.

  • Visual Effects & Movement (Ken Burns Effect)

    A slow zoom-in (Ken Burns effect) was consistently applied to every single image (14/14). This maintained a uniform, flowing pace and successfully brought life to the still images.

  • Transition Effects (Consistency)

    A seamless Cross-Fade transition was used exclusively between every single still image to provide a soft, continuous flow, demonstrating meaningful integration of one consistent transition effect.

  • Text and Formal Elements (Captions)

    Captions were added for the entire duration of the video, providing a full transcript of the narration and labeling all on-screen concepts. (This satisfies the requirement for at least two meaningfully integrated captions).

  • Duration and Theme

    The final video length is 2:08 mins, fitting within the two-three minute length constraint. The theme, "The Caste System," is consistently embraced throughout the production's visuals and narration.
